Amazon Multi-Product Inventory Clearance & Optimization

Inventory Management × Data-Driven Decisions × Cost Control
CASE 01

Background & Challenge

Upon taking over multiple product lines, faced significant stagnant inventory buildup with rising storage fees and poor capital turnover. Several SKUs were approaching long-term storage fee thresholds, requiring differentiated clearance strategies within tight timelines.

Action Plan

Built an inventory health scoring system at SKU level, integrating metrics like inventory age, turnover rate, and profit contribution for tiered processing. Developed differentiated strategies — promotions, bundling, transfer, and removal — while optimizing replenishment rhythm.

Results

Significantly reduced excess inventory ratio, meaningfully improved storage costs, and reallocated freed capital to high-ROI product lines. Established a reusable inventory monitoring and alert mechanism as the systematic foundation for ongoing operations.

Core Value

This wasn't just a clearance campaign — it was building a sustainable inventory management system through data, transforming from reactive firefighting to proactive control.

Inventory Data Analysis Cost Optimization SOP

PPC Structure Rebuild & Core Keyword Strategy

Ad Optimization × Structural Thinking × Data Review
CASE 02

Background & Challenge

Inherited a chaotic ad structure with unclear campaign hierarchy, non-strategic keyword targeting, persistently high ACoS, significant budget waste, and inability to identify truly profitable traffic sources.

Action Plan

Redesigned a three-tier ad architecture: brand defense, core keyword offense, and long-tail expansion. Built independent tracking for core keywords with weekly performance reviews, progressively concentrating budget on high-certainty terms while eliminating underperformers.

Results

Significantly improved ad efficiency, effectively pushed core keyword organic rankings, and optimized ad spend ratio to a healthy range. Developed a reusable ad structure methodology and data review process.

Core Value

Advertising isn't a money-spending tool — it's a growth engine driven by structural strategy and data review.

PPC Keyword Strategy ACoS Optimization Data Review

Product Profitability Modeling from a Financial Perspective

Financial Modeling × Profit Analysis × ROI Assessment
CASE 04

Background & Challenge

Product launch decisions were often based on rough estimates and competitor references, lacking systematic financial models. This led to products that appeared to be selling but were actually profit-negative, while high-potential products were abandoned due to miscalculation.

Action Plan

Built a comprehensive product profitability model covering procurement costs, first-mile logistics, FBA fees, advertising costs, return rates, and exchange rate fluctuations. Introduced ROI and IRR metrics to score each product proposal, shifting decisions from guesswork to quantitative assessment.

Results

Significantly improved product decision quality, promptly identifying and eliminating several hidden-loss products while concentrating resources on high-return categories. The financial model became the team's standard tool for product selection and proposal evaluation.

Core Value

Operations needs someone who can 'do the math.' Embedding financial thinking into operational decisions is what elevates 'selling products' to 'running a business.'
